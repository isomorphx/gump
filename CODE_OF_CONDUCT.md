# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be respectful. Be constructive. Ship code.

## The slightly longer version

We welcome contributions, bug reports, and feedback. In return, we ask that all participants:

- **Be direct.** Say what you mean. Skip the preamble.
- **Be constructive.** Criticism is welcome — make it actionable.
- **Be respectful.** Disagree with ideas, not people.
- **Don't waste others' time.** Search before asking. Reproduce before reporting.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any channel where you represent the project.

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior can be reported to conduct@isomorphx.com. Reports are reviewed by the maintainer. Responses range from a conversation to a permanent ban, depending on severity.

## That's it

No 5,000-word legal document. Treat people the way you'd want to be treated on your best day. If you're unsure whether something is appropriate, it probably isn't.
